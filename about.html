About NayePankh Foundation

Who We Are

NayePankh Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering underprivileged communities through education, healthcare, food support, and skill development initiatives.

Our mission is to create opportunities for individuals and families so they can build a brighter and more sustainable future.

Our Vision

To build an inclusive society where everyone has access to education, healthcare and opportunities.

Our Mission

To empower communities through impactful social programs and sustainable development.

Our Team

Our dedicated team of volunteers and members works tirelessly to create positive social impact through education, healthcare, community support, and empowerment initiatives.
